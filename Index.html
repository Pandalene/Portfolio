What about WordPress?
You might be surprised that CNN uses WordPress, as well as Microsoft News. Blog

Keeping a blog builds trust and keeps your base engaged. A blog can educate, inform, or entertain. No matter what style you write, be sure to keep it specific to your brand. My blog informs readers of current design trends as well as other important web news.

Who needs a blog?
The most important reason for maintaining a blog is to keep in contact with your base. Also search engine traffic is based on new content. Businesses with a blog will usually rank higher on search engines. It also allows you to create content for social media. But the content needs to be relevant to your brand.
